# AI Org Content Brief — Instagram
*For content session: 2026-03-29*

Gideon is making content about his AI org tomorrow. This brief maps agent stories to Instagram post angles.
Hook strategy: lead with the business outcome, not the tech.

---

## Tier 1 — Highest Engagement Angles

### Post Idea: The Morning Chain
**Hook:** "While I was sleeping, 8 AI agents ran my business."
**Body:** 6am: my CTO checks if the website is live. 6:30am: CFO pulls my cash position. 6:45am: Property Manager checks my 4 rental properties for HOT compliance. 7am: CEO sets the day's priorities. 7:30am: Deal Agent counts down to my $50k acquisition fee deadline. 8am: briefing hits my phone. I wake up knowing exactly what to do first.
**Close:** The goal isn't to work less. It's to only work on what matters.
**Format:** Carousel (1 agent per slide)

---

### Post Idea: The CFO Who Never Sleeps
**Hook:** "I hired a CFO. He works for free. He never misses a filing."
**Body:** Every morning at 6:30am, my CFO agent checks cash position, calculates runway, and tracks Mastermind member progress toward my EOY goal. If cash drops below $3k, it escalates immediately. If I don't act, it escalates again.
**Close:** It doesn't close deals. I do. But it makes sure I never go blind on the numbers.
**Format:** Single image or short Reel

---

### Post Idea: The $15k Churn Early Warning System
**Hook:** "One agent saved me from losing a $15,000/year member."
**Body:** My Community Manager agent runs every Wednesday. It reads the Tuesday call transcript, checks Skool engagement, and flags any paying member who's been silent for 14+ days. $15k per member. One save = $15k. The agent doesn't reach out — I do. But it makes sure I always know who to call.
**Close:** Retention is a system, not a feeling.
**Format:** Single image

---

### Post Idea: The Deal Countdown
**Hook:** "I have 18 days to close a $50k fee. My AI is counting every one of them."
**Body:** April 15 is my SBA feasibility deadline on Babia micro resort. Every morning at 7:30am, my Deal Agent checks lender requirements, counts days remaining, flags anything blocked, and drafts communications for my review. I just approve or edit.
**Close:** The deal doesn't close itself. But the agent makes sure I never accidentally miss a deadline because I got busy.
**Format:** Single image

---

### Post Idea: The Ad Gate
**Hook:** "My AI won't let me run ads. And that's exactly the point."
**Body:** I built a hard rule into my CMO agent: no ad spend until (1) site is live, (2) $20k+ cash reserves, (3) 200+ challenge completions tracked. Every Sunday it reports funnel metrics and re-checks the gate. If I try to push it, the agent says no. I'm the one who built the rule — I just needed a system that would enforce it when I'm tempted.
**Close:** The best automation doesn't just do what you want. It protects you from your worst decisions.
**Format:** Single image or short Reel

## Tier 2 — Education / Awareness

### Post Idea: The Heartbeat
**Hook:** "Every agent in my org wakes up the same way."
**Body:** Before any agent does anything, it reads its heartbeat file — a briefing doc that tells it who it is, what it does, what to read, what to write, and what it's never allowed to do. Like an employee handbook, except it runs at 6am every day without complaint.
**Close:** Systems don't drift when the briefing is baked in.
**Format:** Screenshot + caption
