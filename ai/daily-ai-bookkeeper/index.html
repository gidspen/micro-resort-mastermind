What It Does
🔒
Your Financial Data Never Leaves Your Control

The bookkeeper runs entirely on your machine, inside your own browser. We never connect to your bank, store your credentials, or see your numbers. Your data stays between you and your accounting platform — period.

⚡
Set It Once. It Runs Itself.

Connect once, say "run bookkeeper," and your transactions are categorized while you're focused on deals. No spreadsheets, no manual entry, no hiring a bookkeeper at $500/month to do work AI can handle overnight.

💡
Finds Tax Savings Automatically

After every session, it flags deductible expenses, 1099 thresholds, and timing moves that could lower your tax bill. Simple callouts, plain English. Money you would have left on the table.

📋
Full Recap After Every Run

Every session ends with a plain-English summary in your chat — what was categorized, what needs your review, and anything flagged. You stay in control without having to dig into the books yourself.

What You Need
Claude Code

The free app from Anthropic. Download at claude.ai/download. A paid Claude plan ($20/mo) is required to use Claude Code and the Chrome extension.

Claude in Chrome

The Chrome extension that lets your AI agent operate inside your accounting platform. Install from the Chrome Web Store. Requires Google Chrome.

Accounting Platform

Works with QuickBooks Online, Xero, Wave, and FreshBooks. You stay logged in — the bookkeeper picks up from there. It cannot log in for you.

Transactions in Your Platform

Your platform needs transactions loaded before each run. Auto-syncing bank feeds work best. Manual imports? Run it weekly or on-demand.
