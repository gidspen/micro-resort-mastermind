The 2-Day Curriculum

Two focused days. Eight modules. The complete framework to take a micro resort deal from idea to LOI — and the financing structure to close it.

Day 1 — The Foundation

Know What You Want & Find the Deal

Most STR investors never scale because they're looking at the wrong deals with the wrong criteria. Day 1 locks in your exact buy box, builds your deal analysis model, and shows you exactly where to find off-market micro resort opportunities that never hit the MLS.

  • Module 1: Defining Your Buy Box — market, property type, deal size, return targets
  • Module 2: Micro Resort Deal Analysis — RevPAR modeling, ADR benchmarking, pro forma construction
  • Module 3: Finding Off-Market Deals — direct outreach, broker relationships, sourcing systems
  • Module 4: Writing Your Letter of Intent — structure, key terms, negotiating position

a buy box document, a deal analysis model, and a live LOI framework

Day 2 — The Close

Negotiate the Deal & Finance the Project

Knowing what to buy is only half the equation. Day 2 is about closing — how to negotiate at commercial scale, how to structure seller conversations, and how to finance a micro resort without your W-2 income as the primary qualifier.

  • Module 5: Negotiating Tactics — anchoring, counteroffers, concession sequencing
  • Module 6: Seller Psychology — what sellers actually care about and how to use it
  • Module 7: Financing Structures — DSCR loans, SBA 7(a), seller carry-backs, JV equity
  • Module 8: Closing the Deal — due diligence checklist, timeline, deal-killers to avoid

a financing strategy, a negotiation playbook, and your first LOI ready to send

Do I need to already own short-term rentals?

Yes. This training is built for STR investors who have proven the model and are ready to scale into micro resort development. If you own 2–5 properties and are generating real cash flow, this is built for you. If you're brand new to real estate entirely, you'd want to start with STR fundamentals first.

No. You need STR operational knowledge — and you already have that. You understand guests, dynamic pricing, occupancy, and reviews.
