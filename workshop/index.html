What This Workshop Is

You Don't Just Learn. We Do the Work.

Most STR investors spend years wondering if they could buy a hotel or micro resort. This workshop compresses that uncertainty into two working sessions - and you leave with deliverables you built yourself, not a notebook full of someone else's ideas.

Day 1 - Find & Analyze

You Build Your Buy Box. You Run the Deal Analysis. Live.

Day 1 is a working session, not a lecture. You'll define your own buy box - your market, your property type, your return targets - and then run a deal analysis on a real hotel or micro resort using Gideon's model. You don't observe. You build.

  • Build Your Buy Box: You'll fill in your own criteria - market, property type, unit count, price range, return targets - and leave with a document you can act on immediately
  • Where Real Deals Come From: Off-market sourcing, broker relationships, direct seller outreach - the sourcing channels that produce actual hotel and micro resort deals, not MLS leftovers
  • Live Deal Analysis: Gideon walks through a real property. You run the numbers alongside him - RevPAR modeling, ADR benchmarking, pro forma construction - using a template you keep
  • Deal Filter Criteria: What separates a deal worth pursuing from a time sink - and how to know in under 20 minutes

a completed buy box document (yours, specific to your situation) and a working deal analysis model you built during the session

Day 2 - Make an Offer

You Write the LOI. You Map the Creative Financing. Live.

Day 2 picks up where Day 1 ends. You'll structure an actual Letter of Intent using the deal you analyzed - Gideon walks through his LOI term by term, and you customize yours in real time. Then the session closes with a creative financing deep-dive: how to get deals done without relying on traditional lending.

  • Write Your LOI: Work through the term sheet structure with Gideon - offer price, earnest money, due diligence period, contingencies - and leave with a draft ready to send
  • Negotiating Commercial Deals: How to anchor, respond to counters, and maintain leverage through the LOI stage without blowing the relationship
  • Creative Financing for Hotel Deals: Seller carry-backs, JV equity, master lease options, and assumable debt - map out the creative structures that make your deal actually fundable
  • Live Deal Clinic: Bring a real property you're looking at. Gideon reviews the numbers and offer structure with you on camera.

a customized LOI draft and a creative financing map built around your actual deal - not a hypothetical

Common Questions

FAQ

Is this really free?

Yes, completely free. No upsell during the workshop, no credit card required. After Day 2, there is an opportunity to learn about the Mastermind community for those who want ongoing implementation support - but that is entirely optional and never the point of the workshop itself.

Do I need to already own short-term rentals?

Yes. This workshop is built for investors who have proven the STR model and are ready to step into commercial hospitality. If you own 2+ properties and are generating real cash flow, this is built for you. If you're brand new to real estate entirely, start with STR fundamentals first.

Is this the same as the 3-Day Micro Resort Buyer Challenge?

Different content, different format. The Challenge is a broader introduction to the micro resort development framework. The 2-Day Workshop is more hands-on and deal-specific - you'll analyze a real property and build a Letter of Intent during the sessions. If you've done the Challenge, this goes deeper into execution.

What if I can't attend live?

Replays will be available for a limited window after each session. That said, the deal analysis and Q&A components are dramatically more valuable live - if you have a specific property you're looking at, you can bring it to the session and get real feedback. We strongly encourage attending live if at all possible.

Do I need hospitality or commercial real estate experience?

No. You need STR experience - and you already have that. You know guests, dynamic pricing, occupancy, and reviews. This workshop fills the commercial deal gap: how to underwrite a hotel or micro resort, source the deal, structure the offer, and finance the acquisition at commercial scale.

What do I need to participate?

A Zoom account (free) and two focused sessions. The deal analysis templates and LOI framework will be provided during the workshop. Optionally: if you have a specific property you're considering, bring the listing and the numbers - we may be able to analyze it live.

What happens after the workshop?

You'll have a buy box, a deal analysis model, an LOI framework, and a financing strategy - the complete toolkit to evaluate and offer on your first hotel or micro resort. You'll receive an invitation to learn about the Mastermind community for investors actively working toward their first deal. No pressure, no pitch - just a decision point.

How many spots are available?

This workshop is kept intentionally small to allow for live deal analysis, real Q&A, and a genuine working session. Once seats fill, registration closes. If you're considering it, register now - you can always decide whether to attend closer to the date.
