Day 1 locks in your exact investment criteria and sourcing strategy — so you stop looking at the wrong deals and start finding the right ones before they hit the market.

Day 2 — The Deal

Analyze the Numbers & Write the LOI

Day 2 is where you go from interested to committed — building your deal model and drafting the letter that takes you from observer to operator.

Negotiate, Finance & Close the Deal

FAQ

Is this really free?

Yes, completely free. No upsell during the challenge, no credit card required, no catch. After Day 3, there is an opportunity to learn about the Micro Resort Mastermind community for those who want to implement — but that is entirely optional and never the focus of the challenge itself.

Do I need to already own short-term rentals?

Yes. This challenge is built for STR investors who have proven the model and are ready to scale into micro resort development. If you own 2–5 properties and are generating real cash flow, this is built for you. If you're brand new to real estate entirely, you'd want to start with STR fundamentals first.

What if I can't attend live?

Replays will be available for 48 hours after each session. That said, the live experience is dramatically better — the deal analysis, the Q&A, and the accountability of building your LOI in real time is what makes this challenge stick. We strongly encourage attending live.

Do I need hospitality experience to build a micro resort?

No. You need STR operational knowledge — and you already have that. You understand guests, dynamic pricing, occupancy, and reviews. The challenge fills the commercial deal gap: how to underwrite, acquire, structure, and finance at micro resort scale. Day 3 specifically addresses how to close deals without hospitality development experience.

What do I need to participate?

A Zoom account (free), a laptop or tablet, and three mornings of focused time. We'll provide the deal analysis templates, buy box framework, and LOI structure during the challenge — everything you need is delivered live.

What happens after Day 3?

You'll have a buy box, a deal analysis model, and an LOI framework ready to deploy on your first micro resort acquisition. You'll receive an invitation to learn about the Mastermind community — the ongoing implementation environment for STR investors who are actively working toward their first deal. No pressure, no pitch — just a decision point.

How many spots are available?

We keep this challenge intentionally small to ensure real engagement, real Q&A, and a real live experience. Once spots fill, registration closes. If you're considering it, register now — you can always decide later whether to attend.
