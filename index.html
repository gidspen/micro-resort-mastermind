Objections, Addressed

EVERY
Question
YOU'VE GOT.

We've heard every objection. Here's the honest answer to each one.

"I don't know enough about hospitality to build a resort."
You don't need hospitality experience. You need STR operational knowledge — and you already have that. You understand guests, pricing, occupancy, and reviews. The training fills the commercial deal gap: how to underwrite, acquire, structure, and finance at this scale.
"I don't have enough capital to make this happen."
Micro resorts are designed for scalable capital deployment. Modular builds, seller carry-backs, SBA 7(a), and DSCR structures are specifically built for operators like you. Day 2 covers every financing path in detail — including how to close deals without using your W-2 as the primary qualifier.
"Isn't a boutique hotel the safer, bigger play?"
Boutique hotels require $2M+ in renovation plus commercial hotel licensing, union considerations, and full back-of-house infrastructure. Micro resorts are 60–80% less capex per key and generate comparable or higher ADR through experiential pricing — with none of the regulatory overhead.
"The STR market is getting saturated. Is this still a real opportunity?"
The saturation is in undifferentiated, commoditized listings — generic condos and cookie-cutter homes with the same Airbnb template. Branded experience properties — micro resorts — are the premium segment. Growing demand, limited competition, and travelers actively seeking exactly what micro resorts deliver.
"Why is this training free? What's the catch?"
No catch. The training is the demonstration of value. Two days of real content — deal analysis, LOI structure, negotiating tactics, financing — and then you decide if you want to go deeper through our Mastermind community. The free training isn't the pitch. It's the proof.
"I don't have time to manage a project this big."
Micro resorts are built for operational systems and leverage — not for you to be on-site daily. That's the point of the model: a branded, systemized operation that runs with a lean team. We'll show you exactly how to design for that from day one of your project.
