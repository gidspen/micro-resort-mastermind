Who This Is For

You've mastered short-term rentals. Now what?

You know how to find a property, furnish it, manage it, optimize it. You've done it 2–3 times and you're good at it.

But STRs scale linearly. You add a door, you add another unit of work. The margins compress as the market matures. And you're watching other operators build 10-unit glamping resorts or boutique hotels that generate more per door, more consistently, with a real brand attached.

You've thought about making the move. You've looked at listings. You know what a cap rate is.

The gap isn't knowledge. It's the operator-level context you can only get from people who've already bought the specific type of property you're targeting — and the deal flow, lender relationships, and community to get it across the line.

That's exactly what the Mastermind provides.

No. There are no modules, no videos to watch, no certifications. This is a live, ongoing operator community. You show up, you participate, you bring deals, you get better.

What if I can't make the live calls?

All calls are recorded and posted to the Skool community within 24 hours.

Probably, yes. The Mastermind is designed for investors who are already confident in the STR model and are actively preparing for or pursuing their first commercial hospitality acquisition.

No. This is a community, not a product. We review applications carefully to make sure it's a fit before accepting anyone.
